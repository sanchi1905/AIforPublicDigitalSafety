Loan App Harassment

Still Active

Overview

Unregulated instant-loan apps offer quick, low-document loans, then charge exorbitant hidden fees and use aggressive, illegal collection tactics.

How it works

The app requests broad permissions (contacts, gallery, messages) during signup. After disbursing a small loan, the app deducts unexplained processing fees, then contacts people in the victim's phone contacts with threats or morphed images if repayment is late.

Red flags to watch for

  • Apps that demand access to your full contact list and photos to approve a loan.
  • Loan terms that aren't clearly disclosed upfront.
  • Apps not listed with the RBI's registered NBFC list.

What to do if you're targeted

  • Only borrow from RBI-registered lenders.
  • Revoke unnecessary app permissions.
  • Report harassment to the police and to the RBI's grievance portal; harassment of contacts is illegal regardless of loan default status.
