QR Code Payment Scam

Still Active

Overview

Scammers send a QR code claiming that scanning it will transfer money TO the victim - but UPI QR codes work the opposite way.

How it works

Common on OLX/Facebook Marketplace-style sales. A "buyer" says they've sent payment and asks the seller to scan a QR code or enter a PIN to "receive" it. Scanning a QR code and entering a UPI PIN always sends money out, never receives it.

Red flags to watch for

  • Anyone asking you to scan a QR code or enter your UPI PIN to "receive" a payment.
  • Receiving money via UPI never requires scanning anything or entering a PIN.

What to do if you're targeted

  • Never scan a QR code or enter a PIN to receive money.
  • To receive a UPI payment, only your UPI ID or a static "Collect" request is needed.
  • If you scanned and lost money, report immediately to your bank and 1930.
