Fake Job / Task Scams

Common

Overview

Offers of "easy income" for simple tasks like liking YouTube videos, rating apps, or reviewing products, often found via WhatsApp or Telegram groups.

How it works

Initial small tasks pay out real, small amounts to build trust. Victims are then asked to "invest" increasing amounts into a task platform to unlock bigger payouts, which never materialize - the money simply disappears.

Red flags to watch for

  • Any job that requires you to pay money upfront to "unlock" earnings.
  • Unsolicited job offers via WhatsApp/Telegram from unknown numbers.
  • Promises of unrealistic returns for minimal work.

What to do if you're targeted

  • Never pay money to a platform to "activate" earnings.
  • Research the company independently before engaging.
  • Report the number/group and stop all further payments immediately if you suspect a scam.
