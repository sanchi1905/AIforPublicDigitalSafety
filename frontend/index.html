How it works

1Rule-based screening

Every message and voice transcript is first checked against known scam patterns — urgency language, OTP/credential requests, shortened links, lookalike domains, and prize-bait phrasing.

2AI judgment

Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model reviews the message in context alongside the rule-based flags, since rules alone can misread a real bank OTP as suspicious. The AI's read and the rules are combined into one verdict.

3Specialized models for other formats

Currency photos are checked by a MobileNetV2 image classifier trained on genuine vs counterfeit notes. Voice recordings are transcribed with Whisper, then run through the same text pipeline as step 1 and 2.

Know the risks

Common scam patterns active in India right now.

Digital Arrest Scam

Still Active

Fraudsters posing as police or CBI officials claim you're under investigation and demand money or video-call "surveillance" to avoid arrest. Real police never make arrests over a phone call.

Fake KYC Update

Still Active

Messages claiming your bank/UPI account will be blocked unless you "update KYC" via a link, which steals your banking credentials.

Screen-Sharing App Fraud

Still Active

Scammers ask you to install AnyDesk or TeamViewer to "help" with a refund or issue, then use screen access to drain your account.

SIM Swap Fraud

Still Active

Criminals convince your telecom provider to issue a duplicate SIM, letting them intercept your OTPs and take over bank accounts.

Fake Job / Task Scams

Common

Offers of easy money for "liking videos" or "completing tasks" that require an upfront deposit you never get back.

Loan App Harassment

Still Active

Unregulated loan apps charge hidden fees, then harass contacts from your phone's address book if you're late on payment.

QR Code Payment Scam

Still Active

Scammers send a QR code claiming you'll receive money by scanning it — but scanning a QR code to *receive* money isn't how UPI works; it always requests a payment instead.

Deepfake Voice Scams

Emerging

AI-cloned voices of family members calling in "emergencies" asking for urgent money transfers. Always verify by calling back on a known number.

Matrimonial / Romance Scams

Common

Fake profiles build trust over weeks before requesting money for a "medical emergency" or "travel cost."

Electricity Bill Disconnection Scam

Still Active

Fake SMS threatening power disconnection unless you pay via a suspicious link immediately.
